About

Mongeau Renovations is a full-service general contracting company dedicated to integrity, quality, and personalized service. We offer a comprehensive range of services to enhance your home and elevate your lifestyle, always keeping your needs and dreams at the forefront.

At Mongeau Renovations, we pride ourselves on our expertise in various aspects of home improvement, including Plastering/Stucco, Carpentry, Roofing, Landscaping, Flooring/Tiling, Masonry, Concrete, Vinyl Siding, Seamless Gutters, Metal Wrap, Power Washing, Windows/Doors, Plumbing, Electrical, and Decks.

Our goal as a general contractor is to help clients bring their vision to life with unparalleled attention to detail, ensuring that our work stands out in the industry for its outstanding quality and craftsmanship.

Our clients expect and deserve excellence. With years of experience, a deep-rooted passion for our craft, and an unwavering commitment to precision, Mongeau Renovations consistently delivers the exceptional results you seek.

Through trust and collaboration, we strive to build lasting relationships with our clients, ensuring that Mongeau Renovations remains the go-to choice for all your home improvement needs in the community.
